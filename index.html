About

Over the years society has created harsh opinions about what one should look like. This is in many cases is the root of insecurity of individuals. It causes them to feel as if they do not fit in or aren 't beautiful enough. This matter also occurs in Holland. Individuals are struggling with society’s opinion. When they put a lot of effort in looking their best they still tend to get the anxiety of being different than others and it affects their confidence.

R’Beauty’s mission is to showcase the beauty in uniqueness. R’Beautify aims to stimulate confidence by taking the customers facial characteristics and enhancing them. This allows the customer to accept their unique beauty and feel confident in their skin.

My Name is Ruth Dara and I am the owner of R'Beauty. I am a business and HR graduate and a selfthought makeup artist. I live in Amsterdam and have been in de makeupartistry for over 5 years. My motto is "to elevate and enhance". I love bringing out the unique characteristics that my customers already possess.
